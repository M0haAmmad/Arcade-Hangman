Arcade Hangman Rules

👤 1 Player Mode

Guess the word chosen by the computer letter by letter. You can make up to 6 incorrect guesses before you get hung. Use the Hint button if you get stuck!

👥 2 Player (Computer Word)

A competitive turn-based battle! Both players take turns guessing letters of a word generated by the computer. Correct guess lets you continue, incorrect guess adds a part to your scaffold and passes the turn.

👥 2 Player (Custom Word)

Write words for each other! In each round, one player enters a secret word and custom category behind a hidden prompt, and the other player tries to guess it. If the guesser gets hung, the writer wins the round!
